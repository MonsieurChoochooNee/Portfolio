Waste Management Project

This is a project for iGEM Thessaloniki 2023 that i created as a contractor. The application is focusing on waste management, and both clients and companies can use it to buy or sell waste for various purposes.

Live Link

Project Overview

The project is simple, it provides a way for people or companies to get rid of a waste and in turn other companies can purchase it for projects, management or other things to use it. The are distinct categories of waste such as coffee, organic waste, shrimp and crab shells, and more to be introduced. The world faces a growing challenge of waste management, with conventional methods proving insufficient. That is why a platform where you can sell your waste or buy waste was developed by us. Companies can either make some money and in the same time get rid of their waste or they can buy waste to use however they see fit.
